Problem Space

What We Are Solving

01

Food Waste

Unpredictable attendance leads to systematic overproduction in campus mess facilities. Kitchens cook blind, students skip meals — food is discarded daily.

02

Resource Inefficiency

Usable hardware, textbooks, and printed notes are discarded at semester end. No platform exists to bring supply and demand together at campus-scale.

03

Behavioral Gap

Students lack incentive to act responsibly. Right behavior exists but has no reward signal. Without data-driven feedback loops, change cannot be systematic.

Our Approach

The Solution Stack

Module 01

Mess Optimization

  • Meal pre-booking system — plan a day ahead
  • Attendance tracking & predictive cooking
  • Feedback loop for menu improvement
  • Personalized nutritional suggestions
  • Credits for consistent pre-booking behavior
Module 02

Notes & Resource Marketplace

  • Upload handwritten or typed notes for credits
  • Buy quality notes with earned credits
  • Subject-filtered catalog with ratings
  • Peer-to-peer hardware exchange (roadmap)
  • Verified uploader system for quality control

Workflow

How It Works

Step 01

Pre-Book

Log in and reserve your meal slot for the next day. Helps the mess prepare exactly what is needed.

Step 02

Attend

Scan in at the mess or mark attendance via the app. Your slot is confirmed and tracked.

Step 03

Earn

Credits are automatically added to your wallet. Upload notes for bonus credits.

Step 04

Spend

Use credits to buy notes, access premium resources, or redeem for campus perks.
